How to Select A Good Pet


7 factors to consider when selecting a pet

  • Allergies. Some people are allergic to the dander or saliva that comes with dogs and cats.
  • Your space. Some pets need more space than others, and some landlords or management companies don't allow pets.
  • Your life.
  • Your age.
  • Personality and temperment.
  • Life span.
