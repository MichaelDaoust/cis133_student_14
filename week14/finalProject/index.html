Mike's Barbershop in Mesa, AZ

Voted Worst Barbershop in AZ

It all started...

Back in college my friends and I would shave each others heads instead of paying for a haircut. Unfortunately for my friend, the last time he handed me the shaver after removing the blade. I centered the shaver on the back of his head and "BZZZZT!" Just like that he had a giant bald spot on his neck. Ever since then, I knew I was destined to own my own barbershop. My motto: "Changing Lives One Bald Spot At A Time."
